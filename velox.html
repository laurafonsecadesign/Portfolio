VELOX

UI/UX 2024

Project Overview

Velox is a modern financial technology application designed to streamline personal finance management and investment tracking. The UI/UX design focuses on creating an intuitive, accessible interface that simplifies complex financial information.

This project aimed to make financial management approachable for users of all expertise levels, combining data visualization with straightforward navigation to create a powerful yet easy-to-use digital tool.

Design Process

01. Research

The project began with in-depth market analysis and user profiling to understand trends in cycling culture and consumer behavior. Insights from industry reports and competitor studies highlighted a growing demand for customization, sustainable transport, and user-friendly digital experiences—particularly among urban riders and tech-savvy athletes.

02. Information Architecture

Using the insights gathered, I designed a clear and scalable information architecture that grouped products and services into intuitive categories. This structure supported easy navigation across a diverse catalog—bikes, accessories, apparel, and maintenance services—ensuring users could find what they needed quickly and confidently.

03. UI Design

The visual design combined a high-contrast neon palette with dark accents, evoking a modern, tech-forward brand identity. I developed a modular layout system and selected bold typography and dynamic imagery to create a professional yet energetic interface. Custom icons and interactive product tags further enhanced usability and brand consistency.

04. Prototyping & Testing

High-fidelity prototypes were created and tested to validate design assumptions and refine key user flows, from browsing products to completing purchases.
